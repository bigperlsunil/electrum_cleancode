Healthcare

Healthcare is India’s one of the largest sectors in terms of revenue and employment. It comprises hospitals, medical devices, clinical trials,health insurance , telemedicine ,medical tourism, and medical equipment. Two major components of India healthcare delivery system are -public and private.  Public  or Government healthcare system comprises limited secondary and tertiary care institutions in key cities  of India and it focuses on providing basic healthcare facilities through  primary healthcare centers (PHCs) in rural areas. The private sector focuses more in metros ,tier I and tier II cities of India and provides majority of secondary, tertiary and quaternary care institutions. Today Indian healthcare market is worth US$ 100 billion and is expected to grow to US$ 280 billion by 2020.
