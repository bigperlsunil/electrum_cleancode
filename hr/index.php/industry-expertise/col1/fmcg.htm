In India Fast Moving Consumer Goods industry is  growing on an average at the rate of 12 % over last few decades and reaching sales figure of US $49 billion.It is expected to reach growth rate of 14.7 per cent to touch US$ 110.4 billion by 2020 and  17.7 per cent to reach US$ 100 billion  by 2025. Food products,Personal Care,Home Care,Luxurious products and Consumers products are the major categories of products under FMCG industries. Food products is the leading category accounting 43% ,Personal Care -22% and  Fabric Care- 12%. FMCG industries divided Indian market broadly  into –Urban,Semi Urban and Rural market and accordingly  marketing strategies, etc and products are focused. There is substantial changes in consumer behavior in past decade  due to our growing economy, growing income and globalization.
