About Us

Electrum HR is a sophisticated talent resourcing organization, able to delivering compelling resources for organization and helping employee's to find better organizations. As a team of experienced professionals, we understand and cater to the needs of various industry verticals

Along with creating a surplus of resources for organizations, we also open-up the best possibilities for proficient candidates. Through building the long-term client relationship, we achieve first-hand knowledge about the company philosophy and talent overlook. This has enabled us to provide fully-personalized Human Resource services on an inquiry.

With Electrum HR growing to be one of the most frequently accessed HR Provider, we have successfully build a resource-pool for potential employee's. This makes Electrum HR the dream grounds to find your ground-breaking employee and an ideal place to register for your job aspiration.

Vision

With a distinct recruitment and talent acquisition strategy, the Electrum HR aims to elevate itself as one of the top recruitment services in India. Our recruitment process will be focussed on finding the right talent resources at the top, medium and trainee openings for diverse organizations.

Mission

We strive to develop clear-cut strategies to select the optimal talent resource from the surplus workforce. Meanwhile, we also assist individual candidates to fulfill their talent through a truly challenging and fulfilling career.

Our Commitments

  • Understanding the specific client objectives with each Opening.
  • Get to the bottom of talent-pool with respect to each opening.
  • Be consistent with the industry ethics and integrity.
  • Following clear verification procedure with all potential Employer and Employee's.
